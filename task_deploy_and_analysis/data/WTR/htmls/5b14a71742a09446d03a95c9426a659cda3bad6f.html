Comic Creator Priscila Farias

Priscila Farias

(b. 1964, Brazil)  

Priscila Farias was born in São Paulo, Brazil in 1964. She studied graphic design and published her first comics in magazine Interquadrinhos. She moved to Italy in 1985, where she started working as a graphic designer and got involved with magazines Frigidaire and Macchia Gialla. In 1988 she returned to Brazil. She has been editor for magazines such as Animal, and published her work in Chiclete com Banana, Lúcifer and newspaper Diáro Popular.
