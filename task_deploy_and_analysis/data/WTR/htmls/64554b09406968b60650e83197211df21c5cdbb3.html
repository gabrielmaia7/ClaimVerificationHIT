Venice: The Canale di S Chiara towards the Lagoon c.1750-1800

 Post  Tweet  Pin it  Email  Link

  • Description

    A view of a canal side quay in the immediate foreground curving around to the left middle distance, lined by houses, some with awnings; men in groups on the quay and a cat, lower left; the Isle of S Chiara in left distance, mountains beyond.
    Provenance

    One of a set of ten copies acquired by George IV; all ten recorded in Store at Carlton House in 1819 (no 261) as 'after Canaletto'
