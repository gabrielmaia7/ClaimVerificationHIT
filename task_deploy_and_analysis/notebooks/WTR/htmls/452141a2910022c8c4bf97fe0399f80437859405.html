Elizabeth Mary Goold-Adams1

F, #347332, b. 1 April 1918
Last Edited=1 Apr 2009
     Elizabeth Mary Goold-Adams was born on 1 April 1918.1 She is the daughter of Major Sir Hamilton John Goold-Adams and Elsie Riordon.1 She married Donald Maclean Main, son of Donald Maclean Main, on 16 December 1950.1
     She was educated at Sherborne School for Girls, Sherborne, Dorset, EnglandG.1 She graduated from Newnham College, Cambridge University, Cambridge, Cambridgeshire, EnglandG, in 1939 with a Master of Arts (M.A.)1 From 16 December 1950, her married name became Main.1
