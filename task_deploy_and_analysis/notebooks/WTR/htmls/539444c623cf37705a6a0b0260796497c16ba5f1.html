BBC Worldwide Press Releases


04.03.03
Internationally renowned best-selling author of mind- and memory- improvement books, Inventor of Mind Map®


"Tony Buzan's Mind Map Book will do for the brain what Stephen Hawking's A Brief History of Time did for the universe" - The Times


5 Titles Published: 8th May 2003 Price £7.99 paperback


Tony Buzan's books with the BBC have sold over three million copies, and have transformed the lives of millions of people worldwide since they were first published in 1974. His fun and inspiring ideas for improving both the mind and the memory have revolutionised schools and businesses and have been used by individuals to transform all aspects of their personal and professional lives.


Central to Tony's ideas is the Mind Map® which Tony invented with his brother Barry. Mind Maps are relevant to absolutely everyone since the technique offers exciting and dynamic ways of using and improving memory, concentration and creativity. In your personal life Mind Maps can be used for self-analysis, enabling you to gain greater insight into yourself, and to help you examine and solve personal problems. Mind Maps have also being adopted by many businesses and schools. Tony is an adviser to international corporations and in schools his techniques have enabled hundreds of thousands of students to improve their grades and those with dyslexia to overcome some of the issues they face.


Tony's five titles with the BBC, Use Your Head, The Mind Map Book, Use Your Memory, Master Your Memory and The Speed Reading Book, are being re-published in May; new information in The Mind Map Book includes an additional chapter on the role of computers and the latest technology in using mind maps.


Tony Buzan's books have been published in 30 languages and 100 countries. He is President of the Brain Foundation, and founder of the Brain Trust Charity, the World Memory Championships and the World Speed Reading Championships. He advises international Olympic athletes, multi-national companies and leading businesses and is also a consultant to governments, including Singapore, Mexico and Australia. He lives in Oxfordshire.


The Mind Map Book shows how you can use Mind Maps® to transform all areas of your life, from decision making to unlocking your creativity and facilitating new ideas. Mind Map techniques are now taught in school, colleges and businesses all over the world. An illustrated version is also available price £14.99.



Use Your Head: over a million copies have been sold to date. It teaches readers how to wake that sleeping giant - the brain. With practical exercises and useful illustrations, this book is the key to unlocking your brainpower.


Use Your Memory reveals the secrets of training and stretching your memory to the full; and includes substantial sections on studying for exams. It is also invaluable for all those wishing to ensure their memory skills improve with age!


Master Your Memory: focuses on the memorising of facts and figures. Through a special learning technique - the Self-enhancing Master Memory Matrix - you can train your brain to remember up to 50,000 new pieces of information. The principles can be applied in every aspect of the user's life and work.


The Speed Reading Book: offers simple guidelines which show how to read faster and more effectively. Numerous practical examples and self tests help you assess your progress and build confidence.
