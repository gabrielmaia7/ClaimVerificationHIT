Mary Ann Barnard1

F, #377001, d. 1895
Last Edited=21 Jun 2010
     Mary Ann Barnard was the daughter of William Barnard.2 She married Reverend Henry Irwin Cummins, son of James John Cummins and Lucia Swete, in 1860.1 She died in 1895.1
     From 1860, her married name became Cummins.

Children of Mary Ann Barnard and Reverend Henry Irwin Cummins

  • Charles Warren Cummins2 b. 1868
  • Anne Emily Cummins2 b. 1870, d. Feb 1936

Anne Emily Cummins1

F, #377004, b. 1870, d. February 1936
Last Edited=21 Jun 2010
