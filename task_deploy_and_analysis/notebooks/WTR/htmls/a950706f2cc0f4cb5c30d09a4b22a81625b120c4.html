ARNOLD GAMSON
GAMSON--Arnold.

December 30, 1926 - January 17, 2018. Conductor and co- founder of the American Opera Society and Amici Dell'Opera. Former Director of Performing Arts for the County of Westchester. He is survived by his wife, Annabelle, son, David and daughter, Rosanna.

Published by New York Times on Jan. 21, 2018.
